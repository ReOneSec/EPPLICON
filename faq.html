Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and how we work with clients.

What services does Epplicon Technologies offer?

We offer comprehensive digital solutions including Full-Stack Development, Cloud & DevOps Solutions, and UI/UX & Product Design. Our services cover everything from initial concept to final deployment and ongoing maintenance.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex enterprise application could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation phase.

Do you work with startups and small businesses?

Absolutely! We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to large enterprises. We offer flexible packages including our "Launchpad" package specifically designed for startups and new ideas.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with modern technologies including React, Node.js, TypeScript, Python, AWS, Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, and more. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support services. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. Support packages are tailored to your needs.

How do you ensure project quality?

We follow industry best practices including code reviews, automated testing, continuous integration, and regular client check-ins. Our quality assurance process ensures deliverables meet the highest standards.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We excel at integrating with existing teams and workflows. We can work as an extension of your team, providing expertise where needed while collaborating seamlessly with your internal staff.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing based on project scope. We have three main packages (Launchpad, Accelerator, Enterprise) with custom pricing available. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you handle cloud deployment and infrastructure?

Yes, our DevOps team specializes in cloud architecture, CI/CD pipelines, and infrastructure management. We work with AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and other platforms to ensure scalable and reliable deployments.

How do I get started with a project?

Getting started is easy! Simply reach out through our contact form or email us at info@epplicon.net. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project, understand your requirements, and provide a detailed proposal.
