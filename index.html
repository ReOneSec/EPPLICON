FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

What technologies do you use for development?

We use a wide range of modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP, and various cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Our technology stack is chosen based on your project requirements to ensure optimal performance and scalability.

How long does it take to develop a project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide maintenance and support after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. We also provide 24/7 monitoring and support services.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer agreements. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We excel at collaborating with in-house teams. We can integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows, provide technical expertise, or work as an extension of your development team.

Do you handle hosting and deployment?

Yes, we handle the entire deployment process including cloud infrastructure setup, CI/CD pipeline configuration, and ongoing hosting management. We work with major cloud providers to ensure reliable and scalable hosting.

What makes you different from other agencies?

Our combination of technical expertise, business understanding, and commitment to client success sets us apart. We focus on building long-term partnerships, not just delivering projects. Our transparent communication, agile methodology, and proven track record ensure exceptional results.
